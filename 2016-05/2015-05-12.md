- 一部读输入并打印结果的GCD机器
```
(controller
  gcd-loop
    (assign a (op read))
    (assign b (op read))
  test-b
    (test (op =) (reg b) (const 0))
    (branch (label gcd-loop))
    (assign t (op rem) (reg a) (reg b))
    (assign a (reg b))
    (assign b (reg t))
    (goto (label test-b))
  gcd-done
    (perform (op print) (reg a))
    (goto (label gcd-loop)))
```
